Frequently Asked
Questions

Do I need to pay a security insurance? +

No, we don't require large security insurance fees. We only verify your identity via phone and national ID upon delivery.

What happens if something breaks? +

All our kits include full insurance. If an accident happens, you are not liable for the damage. Just let us know and we'll replace it.

How does the return process work? +

It's completely hassle-free. On your last day, our courier will contact you to pick up the kit directly from your home at a time that suits you.

Can I buy the kit if I fall in love with the hobby? +

Absolutely! If you decide to buy your own equipment, we'll provide you with a discount code for our partner retailers.
