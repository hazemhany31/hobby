Identity Verification

We require valid government ID to protect you and our equipment.

1Why We Verify

We require a valid government-issued ID to ensure the safety of our equipment and to prevent fraud. This protects both you and HobbyGo.

2What You Agree To

By continuing with a booking, you agree to:

  • Upload a clear photo of your National ID (front & back)
  • Your data will be used only for identity verification purposes
  • Your booking may be rejected if the ID is invalid or unreadable

3Digital Payments

If paying via Instapay or Vodafone Cash:

  • A payment screenshot may be required as proof of transfer
  • Your booking will remain pending until payment is verified by our team

4Data Protection

Your personal data is handled with care:

  • ID images are stored securely and encrypted
  • Data is never shared with third parties
  • ID images may be deleted after successful verification

Your data is protected under our Privacy Policy. We use industry-standard security measures to keep your information safe.
